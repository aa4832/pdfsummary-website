How it works

Three steps, one report

No account. No server. Documents go directly from your device to the AI provider you choose.

01

Add your files

Select PDFs or text files from your device. The app reads them locally and estimates token usage before anything is sent.

02

AI analysis

Documents are sent directly to Gemini or Claude. The AI identifies themes, extracts data points, and produces structured JSON.

03

Export

Get a polished Word document or PowerPoint deck with executive summary, themes, statistics, and source citations.

What the report includes

AI reads your documents end-to-end and produces a structured analysis.

Theme extraction

3–6 themes across your documents, ordered by strength of supporting evidence. Each backed by specific data points.

Statistics & metrics

Key numbers organized into stat cards — percentages, dollar amounts, growth rates — with labels and source files.

Cross-document patterns

When multiple files agree, the app surfaces commonalities — consensus and contradictions at a glance.

Watch list

Forward-looking items — risks, opportunities, and developing trends identified from your source material.

Word export

Professional .docx with cover page, executive summary, theme sections, stat tables, headers, and page numbers.

PowerPoint export

Dark-themed .pptx with title slide, summary, theme breakdowns, stat cards, and watch list. Presentation-ready.
